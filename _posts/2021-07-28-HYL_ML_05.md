## Logistic Regression

### The 1st step of ML, choosing the function set:

If $P(C_1 \vert x) \geq 0.5$, Output $C_1$; Otherwise, Output $C_2$. where

$$P(C_1 \vert x) = \sigma(z) \\
z = w \cdot x + b \\
\sigma(z) = \frac{1}{1 + e^{-z}}$$
